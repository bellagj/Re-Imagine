What is Bank of Pinball?

Bringing the classic arcade feel back to life.

Beginning with just 17 machines and expanding to over 50, this new attraction in Terre Haute, IN gives pinball a new face. Great for people of all ages to experience, find joy and excitement through a simple metal ball and two mechanical flippers.
